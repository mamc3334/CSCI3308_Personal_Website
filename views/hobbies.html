My Hobbies & Interests

Camping

I really enjoy spending time outside in nature and escaping the business of civilized life. Camping is very peaceful for me and I enjoy being alone or with my friends in nature. I love sitting around the camp fire listening to downloaded music disconnected from the rest of the world.

Soccer

I have played soccer since I was young. I played travel team and high school soccer. Now in University, I continue to compete in intramural soccer all year round and play pick up whenever I have time. I really enjoy the creativity and fitness aspect of the sport and it is a super fun way to stay in shape with friends or just goof around.

Skiing

Skiing is my favorite passtime. It can be anything I need it ot be depending on how my life is going. It can be competition against my friends. It can be an internal competition trying to get better. It can also be a peaceful and beautiful escape just enjoying the bliss of carving a turn. Lastly, it can be a party - sending it big and having fun with friends. Skiing is a super important part of my life.
